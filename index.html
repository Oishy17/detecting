⚠ Tips for accurate readings+
  • Same photo, same light: All standards + unknown must be in one image.
  • Add a white reference patch (e.g. white card) and mark it as "Reference".
  • Use 3–5 known standards spanning your expected concentration range.
  • Keep phone steady, avoid glare, use diffuse daylight or consistent indoor light.
